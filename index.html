Privacy Policy for Hindi Chinese Translator

Last Updated: 07 December 2025

Introduction

We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your information. This Privacy Policy explains how information is collected, used, and safeguarded when you use the Hindi Chinese Translator mobile application ("the App").

Information We Collect

1. Translation Data

  • The App stores your translation history locally on your device only.
  • Translations are saved in a local database on your device.
  • We do not collect, transmit, store, or access your translation data on our servers.
  • Your translations remain private and under your control.

2. User Preferences

  • Language preferences and app settings are stored locally on your device.
  • These preferences are used only to improve your app experience.

3. Device Permissions

The App may request the following permissions strictly for functionality:

  • Camera: Used only for scanning text from images (OCR). Images are processed locally and not stored or transmitted.
  • Microphone: Used only for speech-to-text input. Audio is processed locally and not recorded or stored.
  • Photos/Media: Used only to access images selected by you for text recognition. The app uses the Android Photo Picker.
  • Internet: Required to fetch translation results from translation APIs. Translation content is not stored or logged by us.

How We Use Information

  • Translation Services: Text entered for translation is sent to translation APIs only to provide translated results.
  • Local Storage: Translation history and favorites are stored locally on your device.
  • App Features: Permissions are used only to enable OCR and voice input features.

Data Security

  • All translation history and preferences are stored locally on your device.
  • We do not maintain external databases containing user data.
  • We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information.
  • You may clear your translation history at any time from within the app.

Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services:

  • Translation APIs: Used to generate translations. Text is sent temporarily to these services.
  • Google ML Kit: Used for on-device text recognition (OCR).

These services process data in accordance with their respective privacy policies.

Data Retention

  • Translation history is stored locally until you delete it.
  • Uninstalling the app removes all locally stored data.
  • We do not retain any data on external servers.

Children’s Privacy

The App is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Your Rights

  • Access your translation history at any time
  • Delete your translation history within the app
  • Revoke permissions through your device settings
  • Uninstall the app to remove all stored data

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by updating the “Last Updated” date above.
